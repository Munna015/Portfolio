About Me

With a professional objective to expand expertise and experience in the vast realm of computer science, Mahmudul Hasan is driven to take on challenging and responsible roles that allow him to utilize his knowledge, creativity, research, and technical abilities. He has achieved his B.Sc. Eng. in CSE from the same university. His interests lie in the fascinating domains of Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Image Processing, and Natural Language Processing.
